About

"centre for social Science Research

This section has put the searchlight on the overview of the Centre for Social Science Research, its vision, mission, organizational structure, and collaborations/partnerships. The Centre for Social Science Research is an entity established under the Institute of Research, Innovation, and Development (IRID) within the Kumasi Technical University. The Centre undertakes academic and industry-relevant research. The Centre functions on three pillars, namely; research, teaching & training, and consultancies. CSSR engages in the teaching of Research Methodology, Quantitative Analysis, Environmental Impact Assessment, Land Use, Forestry, Sustainability and Statistics. Some of the research activities undertaken so far include tracer studies, market surveys, project evaluation, training needs assessments, technical report writing and data analysis. The Centre also provides capacity building and consultancy services for organizations and individuals in specific quantitative and qualitative research topics such as instrument design, sampling, data entry, and data analysis (qualitative and quantitative). So far, the Centre has offered training in specialized courses for selected staff of the Ministry of Gender and Social Protection, faculty members of Ghana Baptist University College, Kumasi and Accra College of Education, Accra and Kumasi Technical University; SMEs (example Leather Bags Manufacturers), among others. The Centre has also consulted for MMDAs in the Central, Western and Upper East Regions of Ghana.

Vision of Center For Social Science

Our vision is to become a world class Centre of excellence in Applied Research, Innovation and Development.

Our Mission

Enhances collaboration with industry to undertake Innovative applied research, developmental activities and consultancy services for public and private sector institutions.
