2024|CSSR RESEARCH FELLOW CSSR RESEARCH FELLOW ATTENDS COMMUNICATIONS TRAINING

Centre for Social Research (CSR) of the University of Malawi has this week joined 20 other National Partners from French and English speaking countries across Africa in a weeklong communications training in Nairobi, Kenya. The training, organized as one way of capacitating national partners ahead of the 10th Round of Afrobarometer Surveys to take place in 2024, aims to equip communications focal persons for all National Partners with cutting-edge skills on how to effectively communicate Afrobarometer survey results for maximum impact. CSSR Research Fellow Henry Chilobwe who also doubles as Communications Lead for Afrobarometer Surveys in Malawi is representing CSR at the training.

2024|Conference of R Speaking about the impact of R software in educational sessions

R software has become a transformative tool in educational sessions focused on data analysis and statistics. Its open-source nature and extensive library of packages enable educators and students alike to work with real-world datasets, apply statistical methods, and visualize data efficiently. In educational settings, R enhances learning by making complex data processes more accessible and interactive, allowing students to engage in hands-on analysis and gain practical skills applicable to fields like data science, economics, and social sciences. Its intuitive syntax and strong community support also make it a popular choice for educators looking to integrate data literacy into their curricula.
